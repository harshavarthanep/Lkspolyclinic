About LKS Polyclinic

Founded by Dr. Koushik Lakshmipathy, LKS Polyclinic is driven by a vision to provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality medical care to the Korattur community. We focus on treating the person, not just the disease.

Our facility is equipped with a fully functional in-house laboratory and pharmacy to ensure a seamless healthcare journey for our patients.

Our Specialties

Pulmonology

Expert care for Asthma, COPD, Bronchitis, ILD, and other respiratory conditions. Breathing easier starts here.

Surgical Oncology

Compassionate cancer care and surgical interventions focused on recovery and quality of life.

ENT Care

Comprehensive diagnosis and treatment for Ear, Nose, and Throat disorders by specialist surgeons.

Diabetology
