About LKS Polyclinic

Established in 2025, LKS Polyclinic is dedicated to providing accessible and high-quality medical care. While we offer a range of multi-specialty services, our primary focus lies in Pulmonology and respiratory health.

Our mission is to treat not just the disease but the person, ensuring every patient feels supported through early diagnosis, preventive education, and coordinated care.
